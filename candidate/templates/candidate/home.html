Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create an account on Jobify?

A: Creating an account on Jobify is quick and easy. Simply click on the "Register" button at the top right corner of the homepage and follow the prompts to provide your information. Once registered, you can start exploring job listings or posting vacancies if you're an employer.

Q: Can I browse job listings without creating an account?

A: Yes, you can browse job listings on Jobify without creating an account. However, to apply for jobs or save your favorite listings, you'll need to register as a candidate.

Q: How do I post a job opening as an employer?

A: Employers can easily post job openings on Jobify by accessing the "Employer Dashboard" after registering an account. From the dashboard, you'll find options to post new job listings, manage existing listings, and track applications from candidates.

Q: Is Jobify free to use?

A: Yes, Jobify offers free registration and browsing of job listings for candidates. However, there may be premium features available for employers or additional services that require payment. Check our pricing plans for more information.

Q: How can I contact support if I encounter any issues?

A: If you encounter any issues or have questions about Jobify, please don't hesitate to contact our support team. You can reach us through the "Contact Us" page on our website or by emailing support@jobify.com. We're here to help you every step of the way.
